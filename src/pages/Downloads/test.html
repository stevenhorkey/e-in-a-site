This site mainly consists of various forms and exercises designed to help both you and I grow towards a more fulfilling life. Some posts are 'read-only', but these are the exceptions and not the rule. I've chosen to design the site in this way because honestly, I think there is more than enough quality information on personal development already on the internet. What I see lacking, however, is actionable means to apply that information. This site attempts to remedy that, and to use it effectively, it's important that you understand how to utilize theÂ forms.

HOW TO ADD AND DELETE QUESTIONS FROM THE FORM

To add a question either before or after another, hover your mouse over the space above or below, and press the add button. A new question field will appear when you clicked. Edit and answer it as needed.

To delete a question, hover your mouse or press over the right corner of the white text box. An "X" will appear which you can click to delete that question.

HOW TO SAVE YOUR WORK AS A PDF

Simply customize and fill out the form as desired, and press the page icon on the toolbar to download a pdf for later review.

HOW TO SAVE YOUR WORK FOR LATER USE

To continue working on your form at a later time, download your work using the down-arrow in the toolbar, and when ready, return to the same form on the site and upload the file using the arrow-circle button.

HOW TO SHARE THIS WEBSITE AND FORM

To share any page or form, press the blue plus button in the toolbar. Email or share as desired. Thank you!
